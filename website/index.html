Meet Race Park.

Race Park is a lively top-down multiplayer driving game built right into ChatApp. Pick a car, enter a detailed outdoor circuit, and try to be the last working racer.

The two sides of the screen control the left and right wheels like a tank. Press both forward to accelerate straight, use one side to turn, or run the controls in opposite directions for a sharp pivot.

  • Up to six racers with a different car color for every player.
  • Quick Match or private rooms using an easy three-letter code.
  • Physical tracks with grass, gravel, water, trees, barriers, bridges, and crash sounds.
  • Last car alive wins: collisions and leaving the road cost health, while destroyed cars burn.
